History

People have been captivated with the idea of "capturing" images, places, people, and anything around us. For centuries this was done through drawings and paitings. Yet being able to capture snap shot of the world around us changed the way we see ourselves and how we are able to document life. From wars, to family photos the concept behind image creation has captivated the world for centuries.

  • The First: Camera Obscura (1500)
  • Cameras for everyone: Daguerreotype (1839)
  • Now Made Affordable: Kodak (1888)
  • Compact: 35mm Camera (1913)
  • Instant: Polaroid Camera (1948)
  • Toss it: Disposable Camera (1986)
  • New Age: Digital Camera(1991)
